Our Mission to Reduce Food Waste

Connecting surplus food with those who need it most to create sustainable communities

Who We Are

FoodShare is a nonprofit organization dedicated to reducing food waste and hunger by creating an efficient bridge between food donors and recipients. Founded in 2020, we've grown from a local initiative to a nationwide movement with thousands of partners across the food industry.

Our platform makes it simple for businesses with surplus food to connect with charities, shelters, and individuals who can put that food to good use. By preventing perfectly good food from going to waste, we're helping the environment while fighting food insecurity in our communities.
